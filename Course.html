Course Information

Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Nursing Studies (International)


This two year International BSc in Nursing studies programme is designed for international nurses to progress academically and enhance their already existing skills and theory. It is a level 8 hons degree in nursing, this structure is combined with an appreciation of the individuality of the learner, and support structures for the international student. This programme is crafted to develop a reflective graduate who is self-aware and is able to integrate their skills and knowledge in a wide variety of situations within the healthcare field. It is aimed at potential students who are willing to improve the development of healthcare internationally aswell as in their own country. We encourage you to visit the official WIT website in order to apply for this course if you are interested.

Some more information:

- Internationally recognised academic qualification within a year gained
- Integrated course, a number of modules co-scheduled with other BSc Nursing Programme students.








List of modules:


Year 1

Semester 1

- Introduction to Study Skills (10 credits)
- Exploring Communication and Health Care (10 Credits)
- English as a Foreign Language 1 (5 Credits)
- Intercultural Awareness for Nurses (5 credits)

Semester 2

- Professional Roles of a Nurse (10 credits)
- Management of Care (5 credits)
- English as a Foreign Language 2 (5 credits)
- Advancing Academic Skills (5 credits)
- Enhancing Skills for Practice (5 credits)

Year 2

Semester 3

- Evidence Based Health Care 1 (5 credits)
- Managing and Leading in Healthcare (10 credits)
- Professional Values and Caring in Nursing (10 credits)
- Applying English to Health Care (5 credits)

Semester 4

- Evidence Based Health Care 2 (10 credits)
- Personal and Professional Development (10 credits )
- Health Promotion for Practice (10 credits)

Job opportunities:

Taking part in this course is a fantastic way to progress academically or clinically for nurses within their chosen field of study.
