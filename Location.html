Location


Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT) is a university-level institution in the South-East of Ireland. Waterford is a beautiful city and amazing destination to live/study in. Located on the banks of River Barrow, the city provides great views, a lot of attractions, a city centre full of life and activities during the day aswell as at night. If you have never been to Waterford you're missing out!

Hobbies &Activities


WIT aswell as the surrounding area of Waterford provide a vast amount of Activities/Hobbies to participate in. WIT provides facilities such as the WIT Arena, which includes kids parties, camps, team building & corporate activities. It also provides fitness facilities such as a gym, sports event etc.


Places to stay


Wit provides student accommodation on campus for students to stay. There are also a number of housing estates surrounding WIT where students can also rent out a small house. Waterford City Campus, Priory House Student Accommodation and Trinity Apartments are some facilities worth mentioning for students interested in living in Waterford.




WIT accomodation

Wit provides on campus accommodation. This on campus accommodation consists of 66 Single En-Suite rooms of which 3 are suitable for disabled access. Wi-Fi, Resident Parking, Laundry Facility, Live-in Warden, CCTV system, 24 Hours Security are provided by the college. It is located two minutes walk distance away from College Street Campus and fifteen minutes walk from main Campus. It is an excellent choice for students to consider when looking for a place to stay near the college.
